The Legend

In 1996, Lee Walker, a Culinary Institute of America grad, opened Angels Hollow in Breckenridge. For 25 years, every table had a bottle of his hot sauce. Guests didn't just ask for more. They asked to take it home.

The restaurant sold in 2020. The sauce didn't.

Montañero is that sauce, finally bottled and ready for yours.
